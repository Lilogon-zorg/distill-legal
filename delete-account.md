# Delete Your Account

To delete your Mumble account and all associated data:

1. Open the Mumble app
2. Go to Settings
3. Scroll to the Account section
4. Tap "Delete Account"
5. Confirm the deletion

This will permanently delete your account, dictation history, and all personal data from our servers. This action cannot be undone.

If you cannot access the app, contact us at lilogon.dev@gmail.com to request account deletion.
